== Installation ==

1. Sign up and Login [appsplate.com](https://appsplate.com/)
2. Open your WordPress account
3. Find and Click "Plugins:", then "Add New"
4. Type "appsplate" in the search box"
5. Click the "Install Now" button and "Activate"
6. Wright us to [help@appsplate.com](mailto:help@appsplate.com)!

Any questions?

Feel free to contact us at [help@appsplate.com](mailto:help@appsplate.com)
